Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

  • How do I book a tour?

    You can book a tour directly through our website by selecting your desired tour and following the booking process.

  • What payment methods do you accept?

    We accept major credit cards, PayPal, and bank transfers.

  • Can I cancel my booking?

    Yes, you can cancel your booking. Please refer to our cancellation policy for details on refunds and fees.

  • How do I change my booking?

    To change your booking, please contact our support team with your booking details and requested changes.

  • What documents are needed for international travel?

    Requirements vary by destination. Typically, a valid passport, visa (if required), and travel insurance are necessary. Check the specific requirements for your destination.
